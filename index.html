Hello! I am Mehtaab Sawhney and am a fourth year graduate student at MIT working with Professor Yufei Zhao. I graduated from MIT as a mathematics major with a minor in computer science. My research interests are broadly within combinatorics, probability, and theoretical computer science. I am grateful to be supported by a National Science Foundation (NSF) Graduate Fellowship.

My papers can be found on my publications page, as well as on the arXiv and Google Scholar.
